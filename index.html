About Me

Transforming Spaces Into
Digital Realities

End-to-end digital capture services for architecture, fit-out, and facilities management teams.

I provide end-to-end digital capture services — from 3D laser scanning to BIM modeling — for architecture, fit-out, and facilities management teams across the UAE. My team delivers accurate as-built models and 3D visualizations to accelerate project approvals and execution.

With expertise spanning BIM, GIS, LiDAR, Photogrammetry, 360° Virtual Tours, and Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR), I bridge the gap between physical infrastructure and digital intelligence. My background in Geology and Earth Sciences gives me a unique perspective on terrain analysis and subsurface exploration.

Currently serving as Technical Manager at GEO SURVEY TECH in Abu Dhabi, I lead projects that combine cutting-edge scanning technology with precise BIM workflows to deliver actionable digital twins.
